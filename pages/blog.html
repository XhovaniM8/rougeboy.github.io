2022

A Year in Review

It's been seven months since I graduated from college. Seven months since I left my "set" path in the schooling system (Although I chose to do four years for a bachelor's in Computer Engineering, I do not consider that a choice since it was expected of me and I had no other idea what to do).

Since then, the economy has changed drastically once again which has made work sort of difficult to come by for an entry-level aspiring Engineer. Instead of pouting, I just took the opportunity to work on skills. I went after an audio-visual commissioning part-time job to stay busy and keep my pockets from getting to slim--but I mostly have been planning and going at projects. I have also worked on my creative ideas forming "rouge boy." which will be a platform for my own creative ventures such as fragrances and hosting art.

This is the first of my projects that have come to fruition. Here I can showcase my writing, art, and most importantly my technical projects. That's what a portfolio does duh.
